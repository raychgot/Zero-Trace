Frequently Asked Questions

Is your entire menu gluten-free?

Yes! Our entire facility is 100% gluten-free. Every dish on our menu is safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Do you accommodate other allergies?

Absolutely. Please inform your server of any allergies, and our kitchen will take extra precautions. We offer many dairy-free and vegan options.

Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are recommended, especially for dinner and weekend brunch. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a free parking lot behind the restaurant. Street parking is also available, and we're accessible via LYNX bus routes.
