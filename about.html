A Restaurant Born from Personal Experience

In 2018, Chef Maria Santos watched her daughter Sofia spend her seventh birthday in a hospital bed‚Äîthe result of cross-contamination at a restaurant that claimed to offer "gluten-free options." Zero Trace opened its doors in 2019, not as a business venture, but as a solution to a problem Maria knew too well. We built our kitchen from the ground up with one rule: gluten never enters this space. Our name reflects that absolute commitment‚Äîzero trace of gluten means zero trace of doubt.
